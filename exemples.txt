### 1. Respiratory Conditions
- **Symptoms**: coughing, shortness of breath, wheezing
- **Common Ailments**: Asthma, Bronchitis, Common Cold

### 2. Digestive Issues
- **Symptoms**: stomach pain, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea
- **Common Ailments**: Food Poisoning, Gastroenteritis, Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)

### 3. Skin Related Issues
- **Symptoms**: skin rash, itching, redness, blister
- **Common Ailments**: Eczema, Psoriasis, Allergic Reaction

### 4. Neurological Symptoms
- **Symptoms**: headache, dizziness, numbness, tingling sensation
- **Common Ailments**: Migraine, Stroke, Multiple Sclerosis

### 5. General Infections
- **Symptoms**: fever, fatigue, body aches, sore throat
- **Common Ailments**: Influenza, Mononucleosis, COVID-19

### 6. Cardiovascular Issues
- **Symptoms**: chest pain, palpitations, shortness of breath, swelling of legs
- **Common Ailments**: Heart Attack, Heart Failure, Hypertension

### 7. Endocrine Disorders
- **Symptoms**: excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, weight loss
- **Common Ailments**: Diabetes, Hyperthyroidism, Hypothyroidism
